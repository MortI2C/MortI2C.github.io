About me

I am Aaron Call i Barreiro. Born in Berga (Barcelona, Catalonia) in 1988. I received my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya - BarcelonaTech (UPC) in early 2014.

Systems and software engineer, with strong background on web development and highly interested on research.
I consider myself to be a self-learning person, highly motivated to discover new technologies and always trying to get new knowledges and expand those which I already have.
I like to solve new challenges every day and getting invovled onto projects. This is the reason to keep trying to test all the emerging languages and technologies, since each one of those has a whole new world of opportunities to improve how things are being done.

At my free time I like to participate with organizations on which we are trying to change this world to make it better on the future upon the basis of liberty and democracy.

Experience

2013-2014 - Full stack developer at ConsumerLab (McCann Erickson Worldgroup)
(1 year and 1 month, full-time)

ConsumerLab is part of McCann Erickson Worldgrup, one of the world's largest advertising agencies. ConsumerLab researches how digital era can hep brands to better transmit its messages.

I was developing several web projects, from it's functional requirements to graphic design – co-working with art direction – and it's development. To do this projects back-ends and front-ends were developed with framework Symfony 2 as a core alongside with front-end frameworks such as jQuery, d3.js, highcharts, bootstrap, twig, jQuery Mobile, HTML5 and CSS3 among others. On some projects ASP.NET was used instead of PHP due to customer requirements. On those projects I achieved wide knowledges on google, facebook and twitter APIs. In addition to those projects I also was responsible of managing the internal server of the Lab, with maintenance of the internal applications and improving the employees tools to make this work easier. For instance, an internal DNS was configured to manage the internal applications that employees used and a network file system was set up alongside with a backup system. On the other hand, for some customers was needed to set up new servers on cloud environments with high availability and scalability as indispensable requirements. I also managed MySQL databases with replication Master / slave(s) techinque – 1 master server where data is written and replicated into several slaves –, for data mining processes where it was important to be able to make expensive queries without saturating master server – which was tracking data in real-time (due to requirement) –.

It is worth of mention that while I was working at ConsumerLab, most of the projects I developed belonged to McCann Erickson Worldgroup.
